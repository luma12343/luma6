About Lumatech

Lumatech is a trusted digital VTU platform providing seamless access to essential services like data purchase, airtime recharge, bill payments, and exam pin sales across Nigeria.

Who We Are

Lumatech is a fast-growing Virtual Top-Up (VTU) platform built to simplify digital transactions for individuals, students, and small businesses in Nigeria. Our platform enables users to perform essential services such as buying mobile data, recharging airtime, paying utility bills, and purchasing examination pins instantly through a secure online system.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and secure digital payment solutions that are accessible to everyone. We aim to eliminate delays and make everyday transactions simple, automated, and available 24/7.
